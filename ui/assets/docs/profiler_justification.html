Profiler

Mathematical Justification

The profiler is used to analyze the performance of the optimization algorithms. It measures the execution time of different parts of the code and helps identify bottlenecks.

The total execution time can be expressed as:

$$T_{total} = T_{init} + T_{ga} + T_{bo} + T_{other}$$

Where:

  • $$T_{init}$$ is the initialization time.
  • $$T_{ga}$$ is the time spent in the Genetic Algorithm.
  • $$T_{bo}$$ is the time spent in the Bayesian Optimization.
  • $$T_{other}$$ is the time spent in other parts of the code.
